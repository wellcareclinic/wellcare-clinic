About Wellcare

At Wellcare, we are dedicated to providing the best medical care to our patients. Our team of experienced professionals is here to ensure your health and well-being. We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare, focusing not only on treating illnesses but also on promoting overall wellness. Our vision is to be a leading healthcare provider, recognized for our commitment to quality and patient-centered care.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive healthcare services that improve the quality of life for our patients. We offer a range of services including physiotherapy, rehabilitation, and wellness programs tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. Founded in 2016, we have been serving our community for over a decade, and our commitment to excellence has only grown stronger over the years.

We started with a mission to provide accessible and high-quality healthcare to everyone, regardless of their background. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives the attention and care they deserve. At Wellcare, we understand that healthcare is not just about treating symptoms; it's about building relationships and fostering trust.

Our Services Help You Move Through Life.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy to help patients recover from neurological disorders and improve their quality of life.

Pediatric Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation services for children to enhance their physical and cognitive development.

Cardiorespiratory Rehabilitation

Therapeutic interventions to improve heart and lung function, enhancing overall cardiovascular health.

Balance Training

Exercises and techniques to improve balance and prevent falls, especially in older adults.

Coordination Training

Programs designed to enhance motor skills and coordination for better physical performance.

Myofascial Therapy

Manual therapy techniques to relieve pain and improve mobility by targeting the myofascial tissues.

Gait Training

Therapeutic exercises to improve walking patterns and enhance mobility.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to alleviate pain, restore function, and improve range of motion.

Kinesio Taping

Application of elastic therapeutic tape to support muscles and joints without restricting movement.

Dry Needling

Technique using fine needles to release muscle tension and alleviate pain.

Meet Your Physician

Dr. Swetha Rikesh

Physiotherapist

Swetha is a highly experienced and dedicated physiotherapist with more than 14 years of experience in the field. She holds a Bachelor's in Physical Therapy and has specialized training in neurorehabilitation, pediatric rehabilitation, and manual therapy. Swetha is known for her compassionate approach and her ability to create personalized treatment plans that cater to the unique needs of each patient.

Throughout her career, Swetha has helped thousands of patients recover from various physical ailments and improve their quality of life. She believes in a holistic approach to treatment, focusing not only on physical recovery but also on mental and emotional well-being. Her dedication to her patients and her commitment to continuous learning make her a trusted and respected figure in the healthcare community.
